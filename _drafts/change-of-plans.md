Is been awhile since I [wrote]({% post_url 2024-02-29-why-I'm-turning-my-website-into-a-digital-garden % }) about transitioning into a digital garden back in February, I was planning of moving to a new theme that fits the theme of a digital garden better but after trying out not one but two themes being Minimal Mistakes and Chirpy. I couldn't help but feel dissapointed with them...

Starting with Minimal Mistakes, I found it too feature rich for me, I want something simple and minimal that I can built on over time.

Chirpy was *almost* the right fit but it has certain features and design choices that are more in line for a documetation blog and while I do write guides here quite often is not what this site is dedicated to.  

There's of course plenty of other Jekyll themes out there and I could maybe find one I like with enough searching but I come to the realiazation if I want something *just* right. I should do it myself.

So I'll be sticking with the Minima theme and building on top of it.
